WebGL Workshop Report – Day One

I received a quick report from day one of the free and open Milano WebGL workshop on Saturday, June 6th: it went well, over expectations, with about 30 participants, no-show rate < 10%, great interest and involvement, cf. the Flickr photo album.

EMEA Autodesk Cloud Accelerator Extension

Following up on the success of the first two cloud accelerator classes held in the US, we are delighted to announce our first one-week intensive program taking place in Europe, in Prague, Czech Republic, September 13-18, 2015, to be held at the Autodesk Prague office.

The Cloud Accelerator is a chance for you to work intensively on your chosen project with the help, support and training of the Autodesk Cloud Engineering teams. If you have a project that can make good use of any Autodesk cloud technology API – View and Data, Autodesk BIM 360, AutoCAD I/O, Autodesk InfraWorks 360 or Autodesk ReCap – this is a great opportunity to accelerate your development with face-to-face advice and support from Autodesk cloud technology experts.

A said, this is a one-week intensive program, designed to accommodate the needs of software companies where two full weeks out of the office to attend an Accelerator workshop would be too much of a burden.

To learn more about the benefits of this program, hear first-hand from some of the participants in the first event – a two-week accelerator in San Francisco – on how they benefited by attending, watch the videos.

For more information and to apply, visit autodeskcloudaccelerator.com/prague.

I look forward to being there as well and would love to meet and work with you!
